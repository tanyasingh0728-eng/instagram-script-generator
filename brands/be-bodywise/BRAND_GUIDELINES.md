# BE BODYWISE: UNDERARM ROLL-ON BRAND GUIDELINES
This document defines the creative boundaries, psychological triggers, and positioning rules for short-form video scripting. It synthesizes insights from our Category Intelligence Playbook (Pillars 3 & 4).

## 1. BRAND ESSENCE & TONALITY
- **The Voice**: The "Empathetic Chemist BFF." Clinical but deeply accessible, non-judgmental, authentic, and scientific without being pedantic.
- **Tone Guardrails**:
  - **NEVER**: Sound preachy, use hyper-polished/fake commercial language, or induce shame about body odor/pigmentation.
  - **ALWAYS**: Validate the consumer's daily friction, normalize underarm anxieties, and explain the science with simple utility.

## 2. REVENUE-DRIVING MARKETING CLAIMS & POSITIONING (PILLAR 4)
We win by turning competitor structural flaws (chemical irritation, stickiness, lack of results) into our primary hooks.
- **Core Product USP**: A science-backed, dual-action formula that manages underarm odor *while* treating pigmentation using safe, active chemical exfoliants (AHA/BHA like Lactic Acid & Salicylic Acid).
- **The Vocabulary Blueprint**: Use colloquial, emotionally charged terms that urban Indian consumers use on Reddit/Instagram:
  - Use "*Sweat patches*" or "*dark underarms*" instead of heavy clinical jargon.
  - Use "*Dissolves dead skin cells*" or "*blocks odor-causing bacteria*" to explain the utility.
- **Trust-Building Performance Claims**: Highlighting fast-absorbing, non-sticky formulations that don't leave white patches or stains on clothes.

## 3. PRODUCT EDUCATION & RETENTION SAFEGUARDS (PILLAR 3)
To eliminate post-purchase friction, manage consumer safety expectations, and reduce return rates, scripts must weave in proactive education:
- **The Patch Test Hook**: Normalize that active ingredients require introduction. Frame a patch test as an "expert skincare habit," not a scary warning.
- **Purging vs Irritation**: Educate that a slight initial adjustment period is normal as AHA/BHA accelerates skin cell turnover, but active burning means a pause.
- **Onboarding Cadence**: Visually demonstrate or state the correct usage: *Apply to completely clean, dry underarms after a shower—let it dry for 30 seconds before wearing clothes.*

## 4. SHORT-FORM VIDEO SCRIPTING CONSTRAINTS
- **Zero Ad Aesthetics**: The first 3 seconds must look like a raw, organic personal vlog or a disruptive direct-to-camera statement.
- **Dual-Action Retention**: Pair a vulnerable emotional hook (e.g., "Hiding your underarms in sleeveless tops?") directly with a scientific value drop within the first 7 seconds.
- **CTA Mechanic**: Focus on high-intent community actions: "Save this for your next skincare haul" or "Check the link to fix your underarm routine."
